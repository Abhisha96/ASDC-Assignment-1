Overview
This is a simple and basic restaurant management system.

Limitation
1. It's assumed that every restaurant branch has the same menu like that of the restaurant.
2. It's assumed that every restaurant will have multiple branches and the phone number and emailid of
every restaurant will remain the same.
3. Just one restaurant - branches are added. For rest of them, branches are not added.
4. It's assumed that there is just one restaurant.
5. You can order one order twice.
6. It's assumed that there is only one customer
